1. Measure all-purpose flour and pour into large mixing bowl.
  2. Slowly whisk in milk, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until the consistancy is smooth.
  3. Lightly oil a griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  4. Soak the bread slices into the milk mixture.
  5. Cook the bread slices on the griddle or frying pan. Make sure each side of the bead is golden brown. Serve while still hot.
